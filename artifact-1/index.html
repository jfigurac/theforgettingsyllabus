Artifact #1:
Redacted Documents

Recovered: April 7, 2004
Attributed to: Anonymous


Three artifacts were anonymously mailed to my home on the said recovered date. The three distinct legal documents were wrapped in old newspaper and contained in a corrugated cardboard box, where a note written with black marker reads, “NEVER FORGET”. This particular item has been heavily redacted, revealing verses that seem to read like poems.
